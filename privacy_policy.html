LocalStorage Catcher Extension Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the LocalStorage Catcher extension collects, uses, and protects your data.

Data Collection

The LocalStorage Catcher extension collects the following data:

  • Keys and values stored in your browser's LocalStorage.

Data Usage

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • Analysis and debugging of web applications.
  • Improvement of the extension's functionality.

Data Sharing

The LocalStorage Catcher extension does not share your data with third parties.

Data Storage

The collected data is stored locally in your browser.

Data Security

The LocalStorage Catcher extension strives to protect your data, but no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Consent

By using the LocalStorage Catcher extension, you consent to the collection and use of data as described in this Privacy Policy.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
